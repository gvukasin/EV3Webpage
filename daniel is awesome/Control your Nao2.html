Help


Click the button next to the Camera title to show or hide the images acquired by Nao. Secret: if you click on the video image, you can change the camera you are using.


Use the buttons with footsteps to make Nao move around. To stop movement, use the Stop button.


Click the button with Nao heads to change the direction where the Nao is looking. To center the head, click the center button.


Type something in the text field next to the "Say it!" button and click it to make Nao say it.


Click the actions buttons to make Nao perform such actions.


Under the Sensors title, the sensors indicators can be seen. To start or stop the data acquisition from Nao, click the button next to Sensors.


If you need more information, put the mouse over the desired field and a small tip will pop up giving clues of its use.


Click "Close Help" to leave help and return to the main screen.
